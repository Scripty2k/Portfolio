About me

Hello. My name is Amal. I'm 18 years old and I love to make stuff on the internet. I mainly like making digital stuff. Like websites, music, programs and videography/cinematography.

Scripty2k is my artist name. I produce electronic music, mixing breakcore, ambient, and drum and bass to create experimental and immersive sounds. Besides music, I’m into filmmaking and cinematography, where I get to explore storytelling through visuals. I’m also inspired by travel and the idea of vanlife, and I want to bring all of these passions together in my projects, blending music, visuals, and experiences into something unique.

According to Spotify, Scripty2k is a producer of electronic music, blending intense breakcore, atmospheric ambient, and energetic drum and bass. With a passion for pushing sonic boundaries, Scripty2k crafts immersive soundscapes designed for those who crave deep, experimental beats. Beyond music, Scripty2k dives into filmmaking and cinematography, exploring creative storytelling through visual media. Scripty2k aims to merge these passions, creating projects that reflect his journey, both musically and visually.
